# Dashboard Supabase and Shenanigans

A real-time expense tracking dashboard for MILA, a small business in La Jolla, CA.

## What it does

Tracks business expenses across vendors and surfaces spending patterns through a set of dashboard views. Built to give Mila a clear picture of where her money is going and how spending changes over time.

## Dashboard views

Spend over time — total expenses per day or week

Vendor breakdown — which suppliers are getting the most money

Week over week — % change in expenses compared to last week

Receipt volume — how many purchases per week or month

Average transaction size — per vendor, flags if a supplier's prices are creeping up

Payment method split — card vs cash vs contactless

Biggest single transactions — top 10 highest expenses

## Goal

Ship a live, deployed dashboard to Mila by end of day tomorrow. She should be able to open a URL she owns, see her business expenses in real time, and understand every chart without any explanation.

## Tech stack

React, Recharts, Supabase
